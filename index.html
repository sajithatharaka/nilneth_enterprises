Terrazzo

Terrazzo consists of chips of marble, granite, quartz, glass, shell or other suitable materials. It uses either a cement or epoxy matrix as the binder. Metal strips divide sections, make color transitions, design decorative patterns or logos and allow for movement or crack mitigation.
